Who are we at Alex's Homely Haberdashery?

Here at Alex's Homely Haberdashery we flourish based on the satisfaction of our customers. Unlike the average haberdasher, we ensure that our customers recieve the optimal care available. As a company we seek to grow our comunity and encourage more and more people to see the beauty in haberdashery. We seek to plant our roots deeply into the community and support it the way it has supported us. Alex's Homely Haberdashery is more than just a store to shop for goods, its a home away from home. Where those with distinguished tastes can gather together and support something that they love, haberdashery.

Why should people be interested in haberdashery?

To frequent a haberdashery is to insist upon being a fine gentleman. Here we provide the finest tools that everyone should invest in:

  • Buttons
  • Zippers
  • Sewing Needles
  • Sewing Machines

Investing in a local haberdashery such as this one ensures that the community continues to look dapper and allows them to sustain their own clothing, creating a greener environment.
